Game Rules

1. You are X, and your opponent is O. Take turns placing your marks in empty squares.

2. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the round.

3. If all 9 squares are filled without a winner, the round ends in a draw.

4. Win 3 rounds to win the game. If tied after 3 rounds, the game ends in a draw.
